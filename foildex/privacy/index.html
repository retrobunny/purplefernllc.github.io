FoilDex — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Purple Fern LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates FoilDex, a trading card scanning and collection tracking app for iOS. This Privacy Policy describes how FoilDex handles your information.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

FoilDex does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. We do not use analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. Your App Store privacy label reflects this: Data Not Collected.

2. Camera and Photo Library

FoilDex requests access to your device camera and photo library solely to scan and identify trading cards. Images captured or selected are processed for card identification and are not stored on our servers, shared with third parties, or used for any purpose other than identifying the card in the image.

3. Card Identification

When you scan a card, the image is sent to a third-party image recognition service to identify the card. This service receives only the card image and does not receive any personal information. The image is processed in real time and is not retained by the service after identification is complete.

4. Collection Data

Your card collection data (identified cards, notes, and organization) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your collection data, and it is not transmitted to any server.

5. In-App Purchases

FoilDex offers optional subscriptions through Apple's App Store. All purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how Apple handles purchase data.

6. Third-Party Services

FoilDex uses the following third-party services for card data enrichment:

  • Card image recognition (for identifying cards from photos)
  • Public trading card databases (for card details such as abilities, stats, and market prices)

These services receive only card-related queries (card names, set codes) and no personal information.

7. Children's Privacy

FoilDex is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users. If you believe any personal information has been inadvertently collected, please contact us and we will promptly address it.

8. Data Security

Since FoilDex does not collect personal data, there is minimal risk of data exposure. All network communications use HTTPS encryption. Your local collection data is protected by your device's built-in security features.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The revised policy will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of FoilDex after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
